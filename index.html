My Journey with Type 1 Diabetes – From Challenge to Resilience

My Story

Before my diagnosis with Type 1 Diabetes, life was full of curiosity and joy. Dolls were more than toys—they were companions, confidants, and a source of comfort during uncertain times.

Diagnosis was a turning point. It was scary and overwhelming, but I learned to channel my challenges into resilience. Dolls continued to inspire creativity and hope throughout this journey.

Today, I share this journey through the Doll Revolution Exhibit, highlighting how play, imagination, and persistence can transform struggle into strength. It also showcases my journey to becoming a TEDx speaker, turning personal experience into inspiration for others.

The Exhibit

The Doll Revolution Exhibit celebrates the role dolls played in my life—companions during uncertainty, tools for expression, and symbols of resilience. Each piece tells a story, from pre-diagnosis curiosity to embracing challenges and inspiring others.
