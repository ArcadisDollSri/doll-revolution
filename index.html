Doll Revolution Exhibit

From Pre-Diagnosis Curiosity to TEDx Speaker – A Journey with Type 1 Diabetes, Dolls, and Resilience

My Story

Before my Type 1 Diabetes diagnosis, dolls were more than toys—they were my companions, my storytellers, and my source of comfort. They helped me explore my imagination and navigate childhood challenges.

Diagnosis was a turning point, filled with uncertainty and learning. Dolls remained my anchor, teaching resilience and creativity during tough times. Today, I share this story through the Doll Revolution Exhibit.

The Exhibit

The Doll Revolution Exhibit celebrates the role dolls played in my life. Each doll represents a chapter—from curiosity to coping, to resilience and inspiration. This exhibit shows how play, imagination, and personal storytelling can transform challenges into triumphs.
