The Story Behind the Exhibit

PLEASE VISIT TO EXPERIENCE

Sri was an active and joyful toddler, a phase shown on the ground floor of the Doll House exhibit.

Her Type-1 Diabetes diagnosis brought sudden challenges, symbolized by the darker middle floor , where the dolls in her life became sources of comfort, stability, and courage.

They helped her navigate fear and grow into a confident advocate. Today, as depicted on the third floor , she shares her story through TEDx, BreakthroughT1D, and AnimaT1D.

The top floor shows all the characters standing strong, looking ahead with hope for a cure.

Architects of the Exhibit

The team at Arcadis Architecture firm in Seattle helped Elf Sri design and build this exhibit, bringing the Doll Revolution vision to life. A lot of volunteers and friends worked behind the scenes to prepare the decoration elements.
